# 🛒 **Blinkit Sales Insights**

> 🚀 A data-driven analysis of Blinkit grocery sales to uncover trends in product performance, outlet efficiency, and customer preferences.

---

## 🎯 **Business Objective**

This project analyzes Blinkit’s grocery sales data to generate insights that support:

* 📦 Product optimization
* 💰 Pricing strategy
* 📊 Inventory planning
* 🏬 Outlet performance improvement

---

## 🔄 **ETL Process**

### 🔹 Extract

* Imported Blinkit grocery dataset into Power BI

### 🔹 Transform

* Cleaned and standardized data
* Removed irrelevant fields
* Retained `Item Weight` nulls (not used in analysis)

### 🔹 Load

* Loaded cleaned data into Power BI
* Built visuals to support analysis

---

## 📊 **Key Visualizations**

### 🔹 Dashboard Features

* Sales performance by product and outlet
* Fat content analysis
* Outlet-level comparisons
* KPI tracking (Sales, Items, Ratings)

---

### 📈 Visual Breakdown

#### 🥑 Total Sales by Fat Content

* Identifies impact of fat categories on sales
* KPIs: Avg Sales, Item Count, Avg Rating
* Chart: Donut

---

#### 📦 Total Sales by Item Type

* Compares performance across product categories
* KPIs: Avg Sales, Item Count, Avg Rating
* Chart: Bar

---

#### 🏬 Fat Content by Outlet

* Analyzes outlet-level performance by product type
* KPIs: Avg Sales, Item Count, Avg Rating
* Chart: Stacked Column

---

#### 📅 Sales by Outlet Establishment

* Tracks sales trends by outlet age/type
* Chart: Line

## 🔍 **Key Insights**

* 📊 Certain fat categories drive higher sales volume
* 🏬 Outlet performance varies significantly by type and size
* 📦 Specific product categories outperform others
* 📈 Older or well-established outlets show consistent sales trends

---

## 💡 **Recommendations**

* 📦 **Optimize Inventory:** Focus on high-performing products
* 🎯 **Target Marketing:** Align campaigns with top-performing segments
* 🏬 **Expand Strategically:** Invest in high-performing outlet locations
* 🛍️ **Refine Product Mix:** Adjust offerings based on demand trends

---

## 🏆 **Outcome**

This analysis enables better decision-making by helping Blinkit:

✔️ Improve product strategy
✔️ Enhance customer targeting
